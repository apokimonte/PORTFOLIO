"LOUDS MINDS DONT QUIT"

I’m a digital artist driven by a deep obsession for visuals that tell stories. My work lies at the intersection of music, fashion, and cinematic emotion—where every frame feels like a memory and every project becomes a world of its own. Specializing in creative direction for music videos, I craft visual identities that reflect the soul of an artist or the message of a track. From the first reference to the final grade, I build cohesive universes through carefully curated briefs, moodboards, and design elements that blur the line between art direction and storytelling.
I draw inspiration from a mix of high fashion, underground music cultures, surrealism, and contemporary digital art. My artistic approach is both intuitive and strategic: I listen, observe, and decode a project’s essence before translating it into a powerful visual language. Every detail matters—from the choice of color to the texture of an outfit or the rhythm of a camera movement. This obsessive attention to detail allows me to create work that is not only visually captivating but also emotionally precise. I believe in collaboration, intuition, and a fearless vision. Welcome to my world.
